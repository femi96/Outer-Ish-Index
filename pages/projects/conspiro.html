A Non-Conventional Tabletop

This paper was written and revised as coursework for CMS.301, Introduction to Game Design.

During the spring of 2016, I worked on a project for CMS.301, An Introduction to Game Design Methods. The project was centered around designing a game based on the use of non-conventional skills. The resultant game was designed for 4-6 players with generally positive feedback from playtesters.
